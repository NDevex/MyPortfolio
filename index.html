Project Name: Autocad Plates

Date: 05-09-25

This AutoCAD plate showcases a collection of technical lettering and standard symbols used in architectural and engineering drawings. It demonstrates proper line weight, spacing, and character proportion according to drafting standards. The plate includes uppercase and lowercase letters, numerals, and commonly used symbols such as diameter (Ø), centerline (℄), and angle (∠). The exercise aims to develop accuracy, consistency, and neatness in manual and computer-aided drafting, serving as a foundation for creating readable and professional technical drawings.
